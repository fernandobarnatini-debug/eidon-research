## SHOT 1D — CLOSE UP: SKELETON FACE
Tight close up on the skeleton's huge navy blue cartoon eyes. They don't blink. They don't move. Just staring. Camera barely breathes. Deadpan. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 1E — REACTION: CROWD
Camera pans slowly across the crowd of Greek faces reacting. Shock. Confusion. Curiosity. One woman leans to whisper to another. All eyes on the skeleton in the background. Natural subtle movement. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 2A — MEDIUM: THE RETURN
Merchant slides coins across the marble table slowly. Skeleton slides a vial back. Neither reacts. Neither speaks. Deadpan exchange. Camera holds still. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 2B — CLOSE UP: THE LOOSE ROBE
Tight shot on the loose toga bunching at the waist as the merchant's hand slides coins across the marble. Camera holds on this detail. No commentary. Just the evidence. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 2C — CLOSE UP: THE COIN SLIDE
Slow macro shot of bronze coins sliding across marble toward the glass skeleton hand. The glass hand closes around them slowly. Light refracts through the glass fingers. Satisfying and slow. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 3A — WIDE: TRAINING GROUND
Wide shot of the dusty training ground at dawn. Skeleton on one side, exhausted athlete on the other. Camera slowly pushes in toward the two figures. Golden sunrise light. Quiet and still. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 3B — MEDIUM: THE ASK
Athlete points urgently at the vial. Skeleton extends glass hand slowly offering it. Athlete reaches out and takes it. Stares at it. Looks back up at the skeleton. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 3C — CLOSE UP: ATHLETE FACE
Tight close up on the athlete's exhausted determined face. His jaw tightens. Eyes narrow. He is not giving up. Golden dawn light. Camera barely moves. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 3D — FIVE DAYS LATER: THE RETURN
Athlete walks back into frame looking visibly different. Holds up two fingers confidently. Skeleton looks him up and down slowly. Reaches into the bag. Pulls out two vials. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 4A — MEDIUM: THE FIRST WOMAN
Woman picks up the vial slowly and holds it up examining it. Turns it over. Looks at the skeleton skeptically. Skeleton doesn't react. She puts her coins down. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 4B — ONE WEEK LATER: GLOWING SKIN
Close up on the woman's glowing skin in golden light. She turns to the camera smiling and talking excitedly. Light catches her face beautifully. She gestures off camera toward more women. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 4C — WIDE: THE LINE
Camera starts on skeleton's deadpan face. Pulls back slowly to reveal the enormous line of women stretching all the way around the Parthenon. The line keeps going. Skeleton still hasn't moved. 4-5 seconds.

---

## SHOT 4D — REACTION: WOMEN TALKING
Three women huddle together talking fast. One points at her skin. One points toward the table. One counts coins urgently. Natural overlapping movement. Warm golden light. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 5A — MEDIUM: THE WHISPER
Pale guy leans in whispering urgently. Eyes dart left and right nervously. Skeleton leans down slightly. The exchange is hushed and secretive. Camera holds close. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 5B — CLOSE UP: PALE SKIN DETAIL
Macro close up on the pale arm reaching across the marble toward the vial. Fingers close around it slowly. The contrast between the pale skin and the warm golden marble is stark. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 5C — TWO WEEKS LATER: EGYPT MODE
Tanned guy walks in slow motion through the agora. Camera follows from the front at ground level. Greeks on both sides stop and stare. He doesn't look at any of them. Completely unbothered. Slow motion. Golden light. 4-5 seconds.

---

## SHOT 5D — REACTION: GREEKS STARING
Pan across three Greek faces staring in genuine shock. One is mid-bite, forgot to chew. Another elbows the person next to them. All eyes follow something off camera. 3-4 seconds.

## SHOT 6A — WIDE: SOCRATES ARRIVES
Wide shot. Socrates walks toward the table from across the agora. Greeks part for him. Skeleton sees him coming. Camera slowly pushes in as Socrates approaches. Ominous slow walk. 3-4 seconds.

---

## SHOT 6B — MEDIUM: THE VIAL INSPECTION
Socrates rotates the vial slowly in the sunlight examining it from every angle. His mouth never stops moving. Skeleton watches with arms crossed. Camera slowly circles both figures. 3-4 seconds.

## SHOT 6C — CLOSE UP: SKELETON FIST
Slow macro close up on the glass fist closing. Glass fingers curl slowly. Bone knuckles visible through the transparent glass. Light refracts. Tension builds. 2-3 seconds.

## SHOT 6D — THE PUNCH
Slow motion punch connects with Socrates' face. Cheeks compress. Beard flies sideways. Feet leave the ground. Vials scatter through the air in slow motion. Crowd dives. Epic slow motion impact. 3-4 seconds.

## SHOT 6E — SOCRATES ON THE GROUND STILL YAPPING
Camera looks down from skeleton's POV at Socrates flat on the marble. Socrates looks straight up, mouth moving, still talking. His hands gesture as he makes his point from the ground. Completely unbothered. Skeleton stares down. 4-5 seconds.

## SHOT 7B — CLOSE UP: EIDON ON THE WALL

## SHOT 7C — THE BELTED MERCHANT
