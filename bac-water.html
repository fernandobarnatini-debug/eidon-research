Description

Bacteriostatic Water (BAC Water) is sterile water containing 0.9% benzyl alcohol as a bacteriostatic preservative. This preservative inhibits the growth of most bacteria, allowing the vial to be used multiple times after initial puncture.

Each vial contains 10ml of USP-grade bacteriostatic water, manufactured under strict pharmaceutical-grade conditions. Essential for reconstituting all lyophilized (freeze-dried) research peptides.

Storage & Handling

  • • Unopened: Store at room temperature (15–30°C / 59–86°F), protected from light
  • • After opening: Store at 2–8°C (36–46°F) and use within 28 days
